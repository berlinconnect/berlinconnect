Google Analytics uses so-called “cookies”, text files, that are saved on your computer and allow for an analysis of your usage of the website. The information created by the cookie (including your IP address) is transfered to a Google server in the USA and is stored there. Google will use this information to analyze your usage of the website, to report website activities for the owner of the website and to provide further survices related to the website and internet usage. Google will not associate your IP address with other data of Google Inc. under any circumstances.

You can prohibit the installation of the cookies by a corresponding setting in your browser software; we do, however, make you aware, that in that case you may not be able to use all the functions of this website. By using this website you agree to the usage of your data by Google in the aforementioned manner for the aforementioned purposes.
